Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) 2023 Online Application Form

The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana scheme was launched by the Narendra Modi government to make housing accessible to all sections of the society at affordable rates. PMAY was launched in 2015 and will be executed in a phased manner across the country and the project is likely to be completed by 2022. Last date to Apply for PMAY Scheme and avail subsidy on Home Loan is 31st December 2024. Last date for MIG(I & II) category under CLSS has been extended to 31 December 2024.

Follow the steps mentioned below to apply for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana

Step: 1 -Log on to the PMAY website pmaymis.gov.in

Step: 2 - Choose the ‘Citizen Assessment’ option and click on the applicable choice: “For Slum Dwellers” or “Benefits under other 3 components”.
Step: 3 - Enter Aadhaar card details
Step: 4 - This will redirect you to the application page, where you will have to fill in all the details accurately.

Step: 5 - The details to be filled include name, contact number, other personal details, bank account and income details, among others.
Step: 6 - Once this is done, choose the ‘Save’ option and enter the Captcha code.
Step: 7 - Then, click on the ‘Save’ button. The application is now complete and a print out can be taken at this stage.

How to Apply for the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana Offline

Those who do not want to apply for this scheme online may choose to apply for it offline though Citizen Service Centres. To apply for the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana offline, you will need to find your nearest Citizen Service Centre (CSC)

There are thousands of CSCs in the country and you can find information about the center nearest to you by following these steps:
Step1: Visit https://www.apnacsconline.in/csc-locator/
Step 2: Locate the Common Service Center (CSC) nearest to you by selecting the state, district, and a block from the drop-down menus.

You can then visit the CSC in your area and then seek their help to apply for the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana scheme offline. After that, you can fill the form, provide the required documents and apply for the PMAY scheme online. The form will only be submitted after an e-KYC process is completed. All first-time homeowners falling under the EWS, LIC, and MIG 1 and 2 can apply for PMAY online. If you choose to apply online, you can complete the process for free. On the other hand, if you apply through a CSC, you will have to pay a fee of Rs 25. Please note that the application process for the PMAY scheme is periodically changed by the government and the documents required may also vary from one state to another. Please check the official website of the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs of the Government of India to know the current application process for the PMAY scheme


Documents need for offline PMAY application


  • Copy of ID proof
  • Copy of address proof
  • Copy of Aadhaar card
  • Copy of income proof
  • Certificate of valuation for the property
  • NOC from competent authority
  • Affidavit stating that you or your family does not own any house in India.

How to Track Your PMAY Assessment Status Online

Step 1: Visit the PM Awas Yojana official website.

Step 2: Select the ‘Track Your Assessment Status’ option from the ‘Citizen Assessment’ drop-down menu.

Step 3: You will be redirected to Track Assessment Form. Track your PMAY assessment status by using your Name, Father’s Name & Mobile No. or Assessment ID.

Step 4: Provide the respective details and click on the ‘Submit’ button. The result will appear.
