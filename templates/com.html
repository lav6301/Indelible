Procedures For Community Certificate, Both Online And Offline





Community Certificate






Community Certificate is an official statement provided to the citizen by the state government confirming his/her caste and community.The proof of one’s belonging to a particular caste and community, especially in case one belongs to any of the ‘Scheduled Castes’, ‘Scheduled Tribe’ and the Backward Class as specified in the Indian Constitution.





How To Apply Community Certificate Through Online

Step 1:

Register in the TN eSevai portal and Register for CAN to access the application form.

Step 2:

How to apply e Sevai Registration: Visit TN e Sevai portal and Click on “Citizen Login”.

 

Step 3:

Display the menu page. If you are a new user, click on "New User.
Step 4:

After the Page is appear then Enter the all required details.

Step 5:

You will receive an OTP in your registered mobile number to register in the esevai portal.

Step 6:

registered, log in to the website. Select “Revenue Department”.

 

Step 7:

Select “REV-101 Community Certificate” from the list. Click on Proceed. Fill the pesonal information.
Step 8:

Click on Register to submit the form. You would be required to generate and verify OTP before submitting the form. On successful CAN Registration, the CAN Number will be generated.

Step 9:

First of all above procedure done then Enter the CAN number and search for the records.

Step 10 :

If the applicant is having a unique CAN Number his/her record will be shown in the search results.Select the record by clicking on the option button against the desired record. Verify the details then submit



How To Check Status Community Certificate Through Online

 

Step 1:

Login to TN eSevai portal.Click on Check Status
Step 2:

Enter Application number / Transaction Number to track the status of the application. Click on Search



How To Download Community Certificate Through Online

 

Step 1:

Login to TN eSevai portal.Click on Check Status then Enter Application Number.
Step 2:

Click on the Download Certificate link.
