Procedures For Marriage Certificate, Both Online And Offline





Marriage Certificate






Marriage Certificate is an official document which is issued by the registrar and certifies the act of marriage along with the date and place for the same.In 2006, the Supreme Court made it mandatory to register the marriage for safeguarding women’s rights.



How To Apply Marriage Certificate Through Online

Guide Search

Know your Jurisdiction

Login

  • Step 1

    Visit the Tamil Nadu Registration Department’s Marriage Registration website.

  • Step 2

    select the user registration link on the home page.Fill the personal information.

  • Step 3

    The OTP are generate. After,create username,password,and captcha.select “Proceed with Registration”

  • Step 4

    select “create application.”Fill the personal details. You must now select “Proceed with Payment” and to print the copy of the acknowledgment.

How To Apply Marriage Certificate Through Offline



  • Step 1

    Go to the official website of the registration department, Tamil Nadu The home page will open before you

  • Step 2

    On the homepage, you are required to click on more. After that, you have to click on the search.

  • Step 3

    Now you have to click on marriage. After that, you have to select your marriage type

  • Step 4

    Now you have to click on the search. The status of marriage can use under the status section.
