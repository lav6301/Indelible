Birth Certificate






The birth certificate is the first identity certificate of an individual and a vital certificate in India for various activities. The birth certificate has the date and details of a person’s birth.To get the birth certificate, you must first register the birth within 22 days to the concerned offices or online.After the verification , the birth certificateis issued.

How To Apply Birth Certificate Through Offline



  • Step 1

    To apply for a Birth Certificate Tamil Nadu , you must first register the birth in “panchayat/ municipal/Corporation / Health Department ” in your area.

  • Step 2

    Fill up the form prescribed by the registering authority or as in keeping alongside a certificate from the physician or Medical Institution where the Birth takes place.

  • Step 3

    One has to approach the authority and apply for the certificates on any working day.Following the submission of the form and documents, the authorities will review the records.

  • Step 4

    Authority will be reviewing the details and documents to issue the certificate after accepting the form.
